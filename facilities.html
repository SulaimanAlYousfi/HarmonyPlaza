Facilities

Spa And Swimming Pool


Enjoy our free spa experience, with a stunishing window view to the nature. One of the best experiences a person can have while staying in our Plaza. Harmony and peace, is the sole secret to the best spa. You would not want to miss Harmony Plaza's swimming pool too! Guests at the swimming pool can enjoy free fresh juices and fruits, don't hesitate to ask for one!
Note that the spa and the swimming pool can be reserved privately for a room. For more detaisl check with the reception or send us an email via the contact us page.

Restaurant


Enjoy Harmony Plaza's restaurant, rated as the top 10 in the country! We Supply different food styles so all guests can enjoy the food. We also have a buffet which guests can eat from. We have French Cuisine, Chinese, Japanese, Italian, Greek, Spanish, Mediterranean, Lebanese, Moroccan, Turkish, Thai, Indian, Cajun, Mexican, German, Russian, and finally American Cuisine.
Don't forget to leave a rating of the food picked when leaving the restaurant!

Ballrooms And Halls


Want to enjoy a fancy marriage party? Birthday party? Book a ballroom or a hall, and enjoy the other services that comes with booking one! You can book one in the booking page.
If you want any costume services along when booking a ballroom or a hall, check with the reception, or message us via the contact us page.
